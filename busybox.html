busybox

使用busybox测试网络

busybox.yml

apiVersion: v1 kind: Pod metadata: name: busybox spec: containers: - name: busybox image: busybox:1.28.4 command: - sleep - "3600" imagePullPolicy: IfNotPresent restartPolicy: Always

运行 busybox 的 pod

kubectl apply -f busybox.yml

指定 namesapce

kubectl apply -f busybox.yml -n <namespace>

快速测试

kubectl exec -it busybox -- sh
kubectl exec -it busybox -- ping kubectl.net
